# Privacy Policy for FairDealPro

Last updated: August 3, 2025

## Introduction

FairDealPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FairDealPro mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service:

### Personal Data

- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Display name (for user identification)
- Player profile information (name, skill level, position preferences)
- Team information (team names, join codes)
- Event data (event details, participation status)

### Usage Data

- App usage statistics
- Device information
- Log data

## Data Storage

We use Firebase services provided by Google to store and process your data:

- Firebase Authentication for user accounts
- Cloud Firestore for application data
- Firebase hosting for static content

## Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

## Biometric Data

The app may use Face ID/Touch ID for authentication. Biometric data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
